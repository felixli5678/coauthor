Co Author

Instructions

  • Over Zoom, one player will share their screen with the Co-Author link. That player will start as Editor and all other players will be Authors for the first round.
  • The Editor will shuffle the Genre, Chapter card and the round by clicking on the card.
  • The Authors will use the Genre, Opening Line from the Chapter card, and at least two of the pictures on their Inspiration cards to make up a 45 second story to tell to the other players.
  • When all Authors have told their story, the Editor chooses their favorite and that Author wins the round and becomes the next Editor.
  • Before a new round Authors “draw” new Inspiration cards by clicking on the used cards to replenish their hands and the Editor draws re-shuffles the Genre and Chapter card.
  • First player to win 5 rounds wins the game!
